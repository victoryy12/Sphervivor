Spherevivor is a single-player roguelike, where, as the name implies, you play as a sphere who is trying to survive an endless wave of enemies. Defeat enemies and collect experience to level up your abilities!

__________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
How to Play

Use your abilities to defeat enemies and collect experience points that drop from defeated enemies to level a skill of your choosing. Will you be able to defeat The Absolute Sphere?!
__________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Controls

WASD - Movement
Q or Esc - pause
Spacebar - Jump
Shift - slam attack
Hold RMB - charge
Release RMB - launch
hold LMB - spin attack
